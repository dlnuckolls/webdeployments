Why Choose Us?

Dr. Periago and her team work diligently to provide beautiful smiles and a memorable experience for you and your family. Our practice is proud to serve the people of our wonderful community, and we strive for excellence in all that we do to make sure that you not only achieve the smile of your dreams, but that you thoroughly enjoy yourself along the way!

There are a few things that you should consider when choosing an orthodontist:

Is your doctor a licensed orthodontic specialist?

Did you know that legally a general dentist can also do orthodontics? Is your orthodontic treatment important enough to you that you would choose an office that has a university trained orthodontic specialist that has had an additional two years of training?

Dr. Periago attended the University of Louisville School of Dentistry where she graduated in 2005 with highest honors, Summa Cum Laude. She was subsequently accepted into the orthodontic residency program at the University of Louisville, where she completed a two-year graduate degree specializing in orthodontics and dento-facial orthopedics. Upon completion of her orthodontic residency, Dr. Periago also received her master’s degree in Oral Biology with her research emphasis on three dimensional cone beam radiography in orthodontics. Dr. Periago’s research was accepted for publication and can be found in The Angle Orthodontist journal of orthodontics.

While attending the University of Louisville School of Dentistry, Dr. Periago was recognized with various awards such as the American Academy of Oral Medicine Award, the American Academy of Oral and Maxillofacial Pathology Award, the Excellence in General Pathology Award, and a scholarship for her National Dental Boards Part I score. Dr. Periago was also a Dean’s scholar throughout her dental training and was inducted into the Delphi Society as well as the Omikron Kappa Upsilon dental honor society.

Does your doctor devise a customized treatment plan to meet your or your child’s individual needs?

Some orthodontists give you their treatment recommendations without taking the diagnostic tests (x-rays, photos and impressions of the teeth) that are necessary to plan treatment. There is no substitute for having all of the information that these records can provide, and taking shortcuts can lead to misdiagnosis resulting in compromised results and increased treatment time.

Dr. Periago is committed to delivering the best orthodontic care possible, that’s why she invests in state of the art technology and continues to further her orthodontic knowledge through continuing education. Dr. Periago will always do her due diligence before giving her final treatment recommendations by thoroughly reviewing each patients’ diagnostic tests before devising a customized treatment plan. By doing this, Dr. Periago creates a course of treatment that is unique for each patient resulting in the best and most efficient treatment possible.

Are you interested in the Invisalign System? Is your doctor a Certified Preferred Invisalign Provider?

In addition to traditional braces for children, teens and adults, we are also proud to offer Invisalign, Invisalign Teen and Invisalign Express. Dr. Periago has extensive experience using the Invisalign system. As an Invisalign Preferred Provide, Dr. Periago is uniquely qualified to determine if it is a good option for your overall best treatment outcome. Because of her experience, Dr. Periago is comfortable taking on challenging cases using Invisalign or a combination approach using both Invisalign and traditional braces.

Meet Dr. Periago

Dr. Danielle Periago has long been a part of the Southern Indiana community. Growing up in New Albany, she graduated from New Albany High School in 1996. She then moved to Tulsa, Oklahoma where she received her BA degree in Spanish Literature with an emphasis in Pre-Dental studies from Oral Roberts University. After graduation, Dr. Periago relocated back to Louisville, KY where she attended dental school at the University of Louisville School of Dentistry. In 2005, Dr. Periago graduated from dental school Summa Cum Laude and was subsequently accepted into the orthodontic residency program at the University of Louisville, where she completed a two-year graduate degree specializing in orthodontics and facial orthopedics. Upon completion of her orthodontic residency, Dr. Periago also received her master’s degree in Oral Biology with her research emphasis on three dimensional cone beam radiography in orthodontics. Dr. Periago’s research was accepted for publication and can be found in The Angle Orthodontist journal of orthodontics.

While attending the University of Louisville School of Dentistry, Dr. Periago was recognized with various awards such as the American Academy of Oral Medicine Award, the American Academy of Oral and Maxillofacial Pathology Award, the Excellence in General Pathology Award, and a scholarship for her National Dental Boards Part I score. Dr. Periago was also a Dean’s scholar throughout her dental training and was inducted into the Delphi Society as well as the Omikron Kappa Upsilon dental honor society.

Dr. Periago’s most rewarding job is being a wife and mommy. Dr. Periago and her husband Juan reside in Southern Indiana with their beautiful children. They enjoy traveling, bicycling, gardening, cooking, watching movies, and most importantly spending time together as a family.

Dr. Periago is proud to be a diplomate of the American Board of Orthodontics, as well as a member of the American Association of Orthodontics. Dr. Periago is also a member of the American Dental Association and the Indiana Dental Association.

Dr. Periago is committed to delivering the highest standards of orthodontic care in a warm, friendly, and service oriented environment. Desiring to continuously be the best she can be, Dr. Periago invests in the best technology available for her patients’ diagnosis and treatment. With computers at each dental chair, digital radiographs, and photographic imaging, Dr. Periago is in constant communication with the treatment and progress of her patients. It is her number one priority to treat each and every person who enters the office in the manner in which she would like to be treated herself. As a mother, Dr. Periago wants the best care for her children and she knows that the parents of her patients feel the same way.

Dr. Periago is looking forward to being a part of the Southern Indiana community for a long time to come!
