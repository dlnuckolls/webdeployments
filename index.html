Thank you for visiting the orthodontic web site of Dr. Danielle Periago. Our Orthodontist and talented staff work hard to bring beautiful smiles to New Albany and all of Southern Indiana! Dr. Periago recognizes that a healthy smile leads to a healthier self image and healthier you. Because of this, she specializes in orthodontics for children, teenagers and adults and is also an Invisalign Preferred Provider. Dr. Periago uses the Opal Avex Suite traditional and esthetic bracket system which results in fewer visits, faster results and less discomfort for our patients. We are dedicated to delivering the highest standard of orthodontic care to our patients, using the best technology available for our patients’ diagnosis and treatment such as, digital radiographs and imaging as well as completely paperless charting. At Periago Orthodontics our patients are tops! We take pride in working closely with all patients and parents to ensure an excellent and memorable experience. We look forward to meeting you! Please contact us with any questions and for your complimentary initial exam.

Providing quality orthodontics to New Albany, Sellersburg, Clarksville, Jeffersonville, Floyd Knobs, Corydon and Louisville and all surrounding Kentucky and Indiana areas.
