Thank you for visiting the orthodontic web site of Dr. Danielle Limeberry Periago. Our Orthodontist and talented staff work hard to bring beautiful smiles to New Albany, Corydon, Floyds Knob, and all of Southern Indiana! Dr. Periago recognizes that a healthy smile leads to a healthier self image and healthier you. Because of this, she specializes in orthodontics for children, teenagers and adults and is also an Invisalign Preferred Provider. We are dedicated to delivering the highest standard of orthodontic care to our patients, using the best technology available for our patients’ diagnosis and treatment such as, digital radiographs and imaging as well as completely paperless charting. At Periago Orthodontics our patients are tops! We take pride in working closely with all patients and parents to ensure an excellent and memorable experience. We look forward to meeting you!
